Summary

I am a Technical Support Specialist with experience in providing computer user support in an office environment. After my graduation from The George Washington University in 2015, I began my career as an editing professional. I was hired to edit documents such as candidate resumes and other official company materials in 2016 for a staffing company called MYTA Technologies. I transitioned into managing their online presence via social media and the company website, as well as serving as the primary and sole in-house technical support for the Recruiting Team. Additionally, I also have experience in formatting content (text and images) into print and presentation materials using tools such as Microsoft PowerPoint (with MYTA) and Adobe InDesign (for personal projects).

I am very adaptable and am able to learn new software and hardware tools quickly. After being laid off from MYTA Technologies in May 2019, I have been studying front-end web development focusing primarily on HTML and CSS. I have created this website with these languages to help showcase the skills I am learning. It is based on a template, but I heavily customized the entire site using Visual Studio Code, GitHub and GitHub Pages, the Bootstrap libraries, Flaticon, Google Fonts and other free font libraries, and Google Chrome developer tools. I have extensively worked with other software and systems such as Windows 8, Windows 10, Mac OS X, iOS, Automator for Mac, Terminal, Malwarebytes, Norton Antivirus, Zoom, Slack, Skype, and G Suite for professional, personal, and academic projects. I've worked with Dell desktops and laptops as well as MacBook Pro laptops, iPhones, and iPads.

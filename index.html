Introduction

I am a professional with experience in providing computer user support in an office environment. After my graduation from The George Washington University in 2015, I began my career as an editing professional. I was hired to edit documents such as candidate resumes and other official company materials in 2016 for a staffing company called MYTA Technologies. I worked heavily with Microsoft Office software, primarily Word, Excel, and PowerPoint. I transitioned into managing their online presence via social media (Twitter and LinkedIn) and the company website (WordPress), as well as serving as the primary and sole in-house technical support for the recruiting team. This technical support involved troubleshooting and managing Dell personal computers (PCs) that ran Microsoft Windows 8 and 10 operating systems as well as MFC Brother printers and Cisco SPA504G phones. Additionally, I am skilled in formatting content into print and presentation materials using tools such as Microsoft PowerPoint (with MYTA Technologies) and Adobe InDesign (for personal projects).

I am very adaptable and am able to learn new software and hardware tools quickly. After being laid off from MYTA Technologies in May 2019, I have been studying front-end web development focusing primarily on HTML and CSS. I have extensively utilized tools and technologies such as Mac OS X, iOS, Automator for Mac, Terminal, Visual Studio Code, GitHub, Zoom, Slack, Skype, and Malwarebytes for personal and past academic projects.
